These hand-made abaca slippers are the perfect blend of style, quality, and comfort. Made from natural and eco-friendly abaca fiber sourced from local farmers in Lagonoy, Camarines Sur, they symbolize empowerment and support for rural communities. Crafted with skillfulness and attention to detail, these vibrant slippers showcase the diversity and spirit of our agrarian reform beneficiaries. The soft and breathable abaca fibers provide unmatched comfort, making them perfect for everyday wear or relaxing at home. By choosing these hand-made abaca slippers, you become a part of something bigger. You support the Himanag-Sipaco Development Multi-Purpose Cooperative and contribute to the preservation of their traditional craftsmanship. Your every purchase brings hope and a brighter future for this inspiring farmers' cooperative. Your feet deserve the best fit. You can place orders directly with Anne Villare Barcelon of DAR or Luningning Solo Gaor of Himanag-Sipaco Development Multi-Purpose Cooperative via messenger.
